Game Setup
AI Narration
Generates atmospheric story text for night events, intros, and summaries. Disable for a human GM to narrate everything.
Manual Phase Progression
Phases do not advance automatically on timer expiry. A "Next Phase" button appears on the host panel, giving the GM full control over pacing.
Live Action Feed
Shows the GM exactly who targeted whom during the Night Phase in real time (e.g. "Doctor protected Player X").
Reveal Clue Board Poisoning
When a Logic Jammer acts, the GM panel shows exactly which player's clue board is receiving false data.
Manual Role Prompting
The fixed night sequence is replaced with a role list on the GM panel. The GM clicks "Wake Up" to push the UI to each role's player in any order.
