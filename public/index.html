      📖 HOW TO PLAY

      The Goal: The Town must deduce the exact details of the crime (Who, Where, What) or execute all Mafia. The Mafia must eliminate the Town.

      1. Interrogation: Players take turns guessing the crime. The server grades it: 🟢 (All 3 correct), ⚠️ (1 or 2 correct), ❌ (None correct).

      2. Town Hall: Discuss clues and vote to execute a suspect.

      3. Night: Mafia secretly chats and eliminates. Special roles wake up to use abilities.

      4. Finale: If the final round is reached, players simultaneously lock in a final accusation.
